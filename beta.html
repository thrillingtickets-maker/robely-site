Robely is live in TestFlight — iOS only, invite-based. Here's how to get in, what to expect, and how to give feedback that shapes the product.

§ 01   HOW TO INSTALL
01

Download TestFlight

TestFlight is Apple's official beta testing app. Search "TestFlight" in the App Store and install it — it's free and takes about 30 seconds.

iOS ONLY · iPhone required
02

Request your invite

Tap the button below to request access. We'll send you a TestFlight invite link to your email within 24 hours. Beta spots are limited — we're onboarding in small batches to keep the experience tight.

REQUEST BETA ACCESS

We'll reply within 24 hours with your invite link.

03

Open your invite link

Open the invite link on your iPhone. It will launch TestFlight and prompt you to install Robely. Tap Accept → Install. The app is about 40MB.

Requires iOS 16+
04

Start scanning

Open Robely, create your account, and scan your first piece. The Closet Scanner identifies brand, category, and live resale value from a single photo. Start with something you know — a pair of sneakers, a jacket, a watch.

SCAN → INDEX → EXPLORE
§ 02   WHAT TO EXPECT

Eight surfaces. Real data.

SCAN

Point your camera at any piece. AI returns brand, category, and a live resale range from eBay sold comps.

CLOSET INDEX

Every piece graded: GRAIL vs CORE, cost-per-wear, days since last worn. Your closet as a financial ledger.

TRY-ON ME

Select pieces from your index, pick a model (or add your own photo), and visualize the outfit on a real body.

VOICE STYLIST

Tell your stylist where you're going. Get three curated looks built from your own closet.

REMIX

Pick one anchor piece and get CLASS, DINNER, and WEEKEND outfit variations built around it automatically.

WHAT THEY WORE

Daily red carpet, runway, and street style — each look linked directly back to your closet for remixing.

§ 03   GIVING FEEDBACK

Your feedback builds the product.

Beta testers are the product team. Shake your phone at any time inside the app to send a screenshot and note directly to us. Or email beta@robely.ai with anything — bugs, ideas, things that felt off, things you loved.
